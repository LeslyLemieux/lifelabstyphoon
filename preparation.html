Preparation and Mitigation


Knowing the damage a typhoon has in store, lack of preparation can result in the risks
of having a bigger issue during or even after the store. So here’s a list of things
we can do to prepare for a typhoon:

1. Pack essentials in case of evacuation
2. Have a stock of food and water, as well as medicine
3. Have flashlights, batteries, and radios
4. Tune in on weather updates
5. Stay inside your homes
6. Plan emergency exit points in your house

Some things to lessen the impact of typhoons:

1. Preparation
2. Caring for the environment
3. Assess your home
4. Plant more
5. More sturdy houses




A typhoon is something that we can’t control. But lesser damage can be done if we’re
aware of the things going on around us, educated about it, alert, and most importantly,
prepared.
