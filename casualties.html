Typhoon Reming Casualties


There were 655 deaths, 2,437 injured, and 445 missing reported in the Philippines
due to the typhoon Reming. And this of course also led to several damages to structures
and properties. Not to mention the villages buried due to the mudflows from the Mayon
Volcano. Power and transportation were negatively affected as well.
The damages brought cost millions and millions.
