About Typhoon Reming


Typhoon Reming (also known internationally as typhoon Durian) is a typhoon that hit
the Philippines in November 2006. It affected several areas in the Philippines including
the provinces of Albay, Catanduanes, and Camarines Sur in southeastern Luzon Island,
significant damage was also recorded in Mindoro Oriental, Marinduque, Batangas, Laguna,
Mindoro Occidental and Romblon provinces. Its mph could go up from 118 to 140 mph.
